Royal Enfield in India

Royal Enfield started in the UK in 1901, producing motorcycles with a classic rugged design. In India, Enfield bikes became popular for their reliability and style. During the 1970s, the UK factory shut down, but in 1955, Enfield India (Madras/Chennai) started assembling and producing bikes. Iconic models like Bullet and Classic 350 were revived, making Royal Enfield a symbol of adventure on Indian roads. Today, Royal Enfield bikes are fully made in India, combining heritage and modern engineering.
